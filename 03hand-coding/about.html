What is Genius Weft?>

Genius Weft is an improved version of a traditional hand-tied and machine-tied hair extensions. Characterized by their micro-thin construction, genius wefts are less bulky, thinner and more comfortable to wear, in comparison to any other weft extensions on the market. Genius wefts are also known as micro-wefts, being only 0.4 mm thin. Our genius wefts are designed for the most custom install experience for your clients, since they can be cut anywhere and can be stack as needed, giving you customizable color and volume mix. These wefts work for all hair types - thin, fine thick - and have straight tecture.

Product Details

  • 100% remy human hair
  • 0.4 mm. thin weft
  • 20 grams/row, 100 grams/pack
  • can be cut anywhere
  • silicone free
  • ultra-thin, comfortable and lay flat on the head
  • up to 1 year lifespan with proper care
  • straigh texture
